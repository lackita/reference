Warrior
=======

Barbarians, fighters, monks, etc.

First Tier
----------
- Effort: 1
- Physical Nature: Might or Speed Edge: 1
- Cypher Limit: 2
- Weapons: Practiced with light, medium and heavy weapons; no penalties
- Equipment
  - 2 Weapons
  - 1 Expensive Item
  - 2 Moderate Items
  - 4 Inexpensive Items
